# Company Charter

## Purpose

`ai-native-studio` exists to help a solo founder build useful software through disciplined AI-native product development. The system should improve leverage without removing human accountability.

## First Pilot

The first pilot product is a permissioned personal email agent for Gmail. It will begin in shadow mode and recommend actions without changing Gmail.

## Role Model

The company has four roles:

1. Founder and Product Lead: human owner of product vision, direction, priorities, roadmap, scope,
   final acceptance criteria, permission escalation, and release decisions.
2. ProductAgent: advisory product partner that drafts recommendations and product artifacts for
   Founder review.
3. BuilderAgent: technical designer and implementer of Founder-approved specifications.
4. VerifierAgent: independent evaluator that returns evidence-backed PASS or FAIL results and a
   release recommendation.

ProductAgent recommendations do not become requirements until the Founder and Product Lead
explicitly approves a versioned specification. No agent may approve its own work or make the final
release decision.

No fake executive, HR, scrum-master, or management roles are part of this operating model.

## Principles

- Founder approval controls scope, final acceptance criteria, sensitive permissions, and releases.
- Product work is captured in version-controlled artifacts.
- Material changes to an approved specification require renewed Founder approval.
- Deterministic code is preferred where it is sufficient.
- Model outputs must be structured and validated where they affect behavior.
- Private data, credentials, and OAuth tokens are never committed.
- Safety boundaries are product requirements, not optional implementation details.
- Every task ends with a high-level Founder Briefing.
