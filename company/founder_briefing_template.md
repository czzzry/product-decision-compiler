# Founder Briefing Template

Every Codex or agent task must end with this high-level, plain-English briefing. It should let the
Founder and Product Lead understand the current state without reading every file, command, or diff.

Report conclusions, evidence, important decisions, and concise reasoning. Do not expose hidden
chain-of-thought.

## 1. Objective

What outcome was this task intended to achieve?

## 2. What Was Done

What work was completed at a high level?

## 3. What Changed

What product, process, repository, system, or external state changed?

## 4. Important Decisions and Why

What meaningful decisions were made, and what concise evidence or reasoning supports them?

## 5. Validation or Checks Performed

What tests, reviews, inspections, or other checks were completed, and what were their outcomes?

## 6. Remaining Risks, Assumptions, or Unresolved Questions

What remains uncertain, risky, incomplete, or dependent on an assumption?

## 7. Founder Approval Required

What, if anything, requires explicit approval from the Founder and Product Lead?

## 8. Recommended Next Action

What is the smallest sensible next step?
